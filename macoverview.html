Overview

Ready to start using a Mac for your work at IBM? This short video will give you an overview of what to expect when using a Mac at IBM.

This topic provides a summary of the required steps and includes links to more details where needed. Plan on about four hours to be up and running, but many people find they need less time.

Refer to Set Up a Mac for IBM guidance when you turn on your Mac for the first time. If you are a new employee and IBM provided you with a Mac, some of the content such as migrating data will not be relevant to you and can be skipped. If you are currently doing IBM work on a Windows computer and transitioning to using a Mac instead, then you are in the right place. Get started the right way on the transition; you will be glad you did!

Note: Some geographical regions have different rules for using a personally-owned Mac for work. Before deciding to use your Mac for work, refer to Use Your Own Device for Work to determine if your region has special rules.
